Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Gratitude Diary (the "App") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information. The developer (the "Operator") values your privacy and complies with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

Data Category Purpose Retention Period
Email address, password (encrypted) Account registration and login Until account deletion

2. How We Collect Information

  • Directly provided by you during registration or social login
  • Automatically generated through your use of the App (diary entries, usage data, etc.)
  • Automatically collected through payments via Google Play Store or Apple App Store

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: Writing, saving, and viewing gratitude diary entries
  • Account management: User identification and login authentication
  • Statistics features: Streaks, XP, levels, and other motivation tools
  • Payment management: Processing premium subscriptions and ad-removal purchases
  • Notifications: Sending gratitude reminders and morning alerts
  • Advertising: Displaying non-personalized ads

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as described below.

Recipient Data Shared Purpose
Google (Firebase) Authentication data, diary entries Cloud data storage and authentication services
Google (AdMob) Device identifier, IP address Non-personalized ad delivery
Google Play Store / Apple App Store Payment information In-app purchase processing

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Upon your request to delete your account, we will promptly delete your personal data.
  • Diary entries, usage records, and profile information will be permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Payment records will be retained for 5 years as required by applicable law.
  • Local data stored on your device (notification settings, etc.) is automatically removed when the App is uninstalled.

6. Advertising and Tracking

This App displays non-personalized ads only. We do not track your interests or behavior for targeted advertising purposes.

  • Users who have purchased a premium subscription or the ad-removal product will not see any ads.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs (e.g., Amplitude, Mixpanel).

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data.

  • Right of access: You may request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification or erasure: You may request correction or deletion of inaccurate information.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we stop processing your personal data.
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account at any time from the App's settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly.

8. Data Security

  • Passwords are stored in encrypted form and are not accessible to the Operator.
  • All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Firebase security rules ensure that only authorized users can access their own data.
  • Diary data is securely stored on Google Cloud (Firebase Firestore).

9. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable under local law). If we become aware that a child under this age has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you at least 7 days before the changes take effect via an in-app notice or email. The updated policy will be posted on this page.
