5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Upon your request to delete your account, we will promptly delete your personal data.
  • Diary entries, usage records, and profile information will be permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Payment records will be retained for 5 years as required by applicable law.
  • Local data stored on your device (notification settings, etc.) is automatically removed when the App is uninstalled.

6. AI Feature Consent (Premium Only)

Premium subscribers who wish to use AI Insights and Gratitude Recommendations must provide separate consent for their diary text to be sent to Google LLC (Gemini API).

  • When consent is obtained: At premium subscription checkout, or at first use of AI features
  • Data transferred: Gratitude diary text, user display name (for personalization)
  • Purpose: Generation of monthly AI insights and daily gratitude recommendations
  • Processing: The Gemini API does not use transmitted data for model training (Google's official policy).
  • Retention: Data is transmitted transiently during the API call. Generated responses are stored in the user's Firestore document (users/{uid}/recommendations, users/{uid}/insights) and are deleted when the account is deleted.
  • Revocation: You may revoke consent at any time via [Settings > Revoke AI Feature Consent] inside the App.

AI feature consent applies only to premium AI insights and recommendations via the Gemini API. Even if you decline or revoke consent, the following features remain available:

  • Writing, saving, and viewing gratitude diary entries
  • Automated sentiment and noun analysis with charts (via Google Cloud Natural Language)
  • Premium subscription and ad-removal benefits
  • Streaks, XP, levels, and statistics features

7. Advertising and Tracking

This App displays non-personalized ads only. We do not track your interests or behavior for targeted advertising purposes.

  • Users who have purchased a premium subscription or the ad-removal product will not see any ads.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs (e.g., Amplitude, Mixpanel).
  • Google Firebase Analytics is used solely for app quality improvement analytics and is not used for personalized advertising tracking.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data.

  • Right of access: You may request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification or erasure: You may request correction or deletion of inaccurate information.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we stop processing your personal data.
  • AI feature consent revocation: You may revoke AI feature consent instantly at Settings > Revoke AI Feature Consent.
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account at any time from the App's settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly.

9. Data Security

  • Passwords are stored in encrypted form and are not accessible to the Operator.
  • All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Firebase security rules ensure that only authorized users can access their own data.
  • Diary data is securely stored on Google Cloud (Firebase Firestore).

10. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable under local law). If we become aware that a child under this age has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. Premium subscription and AI features are available only to users aged 14 and older.
